# Renewable Energy Trading Platform

## Overview

A peer-to-peer renewable energy trading platform built on blockchain technology that enables direct energy transactions between producers and consumers while ensuring transparency, efficiency, and sustainable energy practices.

## System Architecture

The platform consists of two core smart contracts:

### Energy Token Contract
- **Purpose**: Tokenized representation of renewable energy units
- **Features**: 
  - Mint energy tokens based on verified renewable energy production
  - Transfer tokens between users for energy trading
  - Burn tokens upon energy consumption
  - Track energy source verification and metadata

### Grid Settlement Contract
- **Purpose**: Automated settlement system for energy transactions
- **Features**:
  - Automated matching of energy buyers and sellers
  - Real-time pricing mechanisms
  - Settlement of payments and energy transfers
  - Grid stability monitoring and balancing

## Key Features

- **Decentralized Trading**: Direct peer-to-peer energy transactions without intermediaries
- **Renewable Verification**: Smart contract-based verification of renewable energy sources
- **Automated Settlement**: Real-time transaction processing and settlement
- **Grid Integration**: Seamless integration with existing power grid infrastructure
- **Transparent Pricing**: Market-driven pricing mechanisms with full transparency
- **Environmental Impact**: Track and incentivize renewable energy production

## Smart Contract Architecture

The system utilizes Clarity smart contracts deployed on the Stacks blockchain:

- `energy-token.clar`: Manages tokenization of energy units
- `grid-settlement.clar`: Handles automated trading and settlement
